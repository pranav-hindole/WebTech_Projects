SARDAR VALLABHBHAI PATEL
Great Indian Politician who served as the first Deputy Prime Minister of India.

IRON MAN OF INDIA

  • Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel was born on 31st October 1875 in Nadiad city of Gujarat.

  • Sardar Patel earned his degree in law of England and returned India to practice in Gujarat.

  • He was against British Goverment and so refused all the posts offered by them.

  • Patel married Jhaverben Patel in 1891 and they had two children.

  • Sardar Patel was deeply Affected by Mahatma gandhi and became a follower of Gandhian principles.

  • He led "No Tax Campaign" for farmers in 1918 which was a peaceful campaign.

  • He was also imprisoned in 1930 for participatiing in "Salt Satyagrah".

  • In 1931, Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel was elected the president of “Indain National Congress.”

  • Patel was the first Deputy Prime Minister of India and served from 15th August 1947 to 15 December 1950.

  • Sardar Patel was awarded "Bharat Ratna"posthumously in 1991.

  • At the time of partition of India, Sardar Patel was representing India in Partition Council .

  • Due to this iron will ,Sradar Patel was called the "Iron Man"of India.

  • Saradar Patel was the leading personality in Constituent Assembly.

  • Sardar Patel played a lead role in integrating the princely states to India.

  • Sardar Patel was considered to be the father of "All India Services".

  • The tallest statue of Sardar Patel is in Gujarat name "Statue Of Unity"and is also tallest statue in world.

  • Sardar Patel was not only a true Patriot by the mind ,word and deed but also was a great supporter of Indian Culture.

  • Sardar Patel left the world on 15th December 1950 due to massive heart attack.
